Why it works

RSVP

When reading a page, your eyes jump between words in rapid movements called saccades — each costing 20-30ms where your brain goes idle. RSVP eliminates saccades entirely. Words come to you at a fixed point, so 100% of your attention goes to comprehension. The red letter marks the Optimal Recognition Point — where your brain identifies words fastest.
